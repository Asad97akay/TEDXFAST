TEDx Speakers

The theme for TEDxFAST is Starting Up the Right Way.

Everyone is talking about entrepreneurship and start up these days. They’re the buzzword in our modern society. What exactly do these terms mean? What are the challenges of starting one? What are the dangers of maintaining a start-up? Is it better to persist and iron it out, or quit and save whatever you can in terms of your pride and money? What mindset is required for starting one? What are the myths surrounding this venture?

We hope to answer questions of such nature when it comes to such a diverse and innovate field. Our speakers will be highly respected individuals with an abundance of experience. Some have been successful, some haven’t, but the underlying point is that they each will be having something unique and insightful to offer. So sit back, relax and prepare to be blown away.

FAIZAN LAGHARI

"Faizan Laghari is a serial-entrepreneur with 14 years of experience and counting, in the business world. A techie at heart, Faizan also created Pakistan's 1st Co-working space, Suite401 and has since launched Forrun, an instant delivery and last-mile logistics company, in addition to 4 other businesses in this time period, 3 of which he exited (businesses got acquired by a larger firm) a couple of years ago including Forrun, and is already working on new ideas for the future already."

SANA KHAN NIAZI

"Sana Khan Niazi is an Entrepreneur, a Theatre actor and a Stand-up comedian. Growing up as a shy kid, she used to think a lot of how she did not want to be invincible. Every thing she does has one purpose: to break – stereotypes, poverty, her fears, and ridiculous opinions.. A full time entrepreneur and kicking ass at it so she thought why not try being funny too. Every day for her is a new opportunity to learn, to evolve and a new challenge to overcome. Her entrepreneurial journey has been interestingly versatile so far. With her brand, Paimona, she aims to revive our centuries old crafts with modern design while creating a sustainable living for the craftsmen through training, education and exposure to global aesthetic."

About TEDx

In the spirit of ideas worth spreading, TED has created a program called TEDx. TEDx is a program of local, self-organized events that bring people together to share a TED-like experience. Our event is called TEDxFAST, where x = independently organized TED event. At our TEDxFAST event, TEDTalks video and live speakers will combine to spark deep discussion and connection in a small group. The TED Conference provides general guidance for the TEDx program, but individual TEDx events, including ours, are self-organized. Learn more about TEDx at TEDx Talks.
